Welcome to Chechela's

We are Lesotho's trusted sheep supplier, specializing in providing high-quality sheep breeds to farmers and households across all districts. Located in the beautiful Mokhotlong Sani Pass, we deliver fresh, healthy sheep right to your doorstep.

Our Startup Story

Chechela's Sheep Supplier began with a simple vision: to provide Lesotho families with healthy, quality sheep while supporting local farmers. What started as a small family farm in Mokhotlong has grown into a trusted supplier serving customers across all districts. Our journey reflects our commitment to quality, community, and sustainable farming practices.

Today, we proudly deliver free to any district in Lesotho, ensuring that every family can access the best sheep for their needs.
