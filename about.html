About the Startup

Our mission, vision, and the story behind Chechela's

Our Mission

To provide every household in Lesotho with access to quality, healthy sheep at affordable prices, while supporting sustainable farming practices in our community.

Our Vision

To become Lesotho's most trusted sheep supplier, recognized for quality, reliability, and commitment to customer satisfaction across all districts.

The Startup Story

Chechela's Sheep Supplier was founded in Mokhotlong, Sani Pass, by a passionate farmer who saw the need for reliable sheep supply across Lesotho. Starting with just 20 sheep, we've grown through dedication, hard work, and a commitment to quality. Today, we deliver to all districts including Maseru, Leribe, Berea, Mafeteng, Mohale's Hoek, Quthing, Qacha's Nek, Mokhotlong, Thaba-Tseka, and Butha-Buthe.

Our free delivery service ensures that no matter where you are in Lesotho, you can access our quality sheep. We work directly with local farmers to support the community and ensure fair practices throughout our supply chain.

Our Location

📍 Mokhotlong, Sani Pass
Nestled in the beautiful mountains of Lesotho, our farm benefits from the fresh mountain air and natural grazing lands that contribute to the health and quality of our sheep.
