How We Operate

Chechela's Sheep Supplier operates on a direct-to-consumer model. We raise our sheep on our farm in Mokhotlong, and deliver directly to customers across all districts of Lesotho. By eliminating middlemen, we ensure competitive prices and maintain quality control throughout the process.

Our Value Proposition

  • ✓ Quality sheep breeds from trusted sources
  • ✓ Free delivery to any district in Lesotho
  • ✓ Competitive pricing with no hidden fees
  • ✓ Support for local farmers and community
  • ✓ Healthy, well-cared-for animals

Revenue Streams

  • • Direct sheep sales to households and farmers
  • • Bulk orders for events and ceremonies
  • • Breeding stock for other farmers
  • • Wool sales (seasonal)

Market Reach

We serve customers across all districts of Lesotho:

  • • Maseru District
  • • Leribe District
  • • Berea District
  • • Mafeteng District
  • • Mohale's Hoek District
  • • Quthing District

  • • Butha-Buthe District
